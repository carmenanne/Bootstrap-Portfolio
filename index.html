About Me

Originally from the Hoosier state (that's Indiana for those unfamiliar with the Midwest), I relocated to California after completing my Master's degree. While the peninsula was my first home, I love living in San Francisco now and being a part of the local community.

In addition to working full time I also volunteer as an adult cheerleader with CHEER San Francisco. You heard that correctly - adult, volunteer cheerleaders - and we raise money for local charities that support those suffering from life-challenging illnesses.

When I'm not working, cheering, or coding you can find me running through Goldent Gate park, sampling adult beverages about town, or binge-watching shows on Netflix.
